# FlashSpace RAG Knowledge Base

## 1. Overview

FlashSpace is a digital platform that provides flexible workspace solutions for individuals, startups, and businesses. The platform connects workspace seekers with space providers and offers multiple services including coworking spaces, virtual offices, seat bookings, and meeting rooms.

FlashSpace operates as a marketplace where users can explore, book, and manage workspaces across different cities.

---

# 2. Core Services

FlashSpace offers the following primary services.

## 2.1 Virtual Office

A Virtual Office provides a professional business address without requiring a physical workspace.

Typical use cases:

* Company registration
* GST registration
* Professional mailing address
* Business correspondence

Key features:

* Business address
* Mail handling
* GST registration support
* Meeting room access (optional)

Typical pricing:

Rs 1,500 - Rs 5,000 per month depending on location.

---

## 2.2 Coworking Space

Coworking spaces are shared work environments designed for professionals, freelancers, and startups.

Types of coworking options:

* Hot Desk
* Dedicated Desk
* Private Cabin

Features may include:

* High-speed WiFi
* Office infrastructure
* Community networking
* Meeting rooms
* Reception services

Typical pricing:

Rs 4,000 - Rs 12,000 per seat per month.

---

## 2.3 Seat Booking

Seat booking allows users to reserve individual desks for a limited time.

Common booking durations:

* Daily
* Weekly
* Monthly

Seat booking is typically used by:

* freelancers
* remote workers
* traveling professionals

---

## 2.4 Meeting Rooms

Meeting rooms are reservable spaces used for meetings, presentations, and client discussions.

Common use cases:

* team meetings
* interviews
* client meetings
* presentations

Pricing model:

Usually hourly or half-day based.

Typical price range:

Rs 300 - Rs 1,500 per hour depending on city and facility.

---

# 3. Platform Roles

FlashSpace platform supports multiple user roles.

## 3.1 Guest

Guests are visitors who have not logged in.

Guests can:

* explore workspaces
* view pricing ranges
* check locations
* browse amenities

Guests cannot:

* confirm bookings
* view personal bookings
* access invoices

Guests must create an account before booking.

---

## 3.2 Registered User (Customer)

Registered users can:

* search workspaces
* book workspaces
* view bookings
* cancel bookings
* download invoices
* view payment status

Users can book:

* coworking desks
* meeting rooms
* virtual offices

---

## 3.3 Space Partner

Space partners are workspace owners who list their spaces on FlashSpace.

Partners can:

* list coworking spaces
* manage availability
* update pricing
* track bookings
* view revenue

Partners earn revenue from bookings made through FlashSpace.

---

## 3.4 Affiliate Partner

Affiliate partners promote FlashSpace services using referral links.

Affiliates can:

* generate referral links
* track leads
* monitor conversions
* earn commissions

Commissions are paid when a referred user successfully books a workspace.

---

## 3.5 Admin

Admins manage the overall FlashSpace platform.

Admins can access:

* user analytics
* booking statistics
* revenue reports
* partner performance
* affiliate performance
* support tickets

Admins also monitor system health and compliance.

---

# 4. Workspace Attributes

Each workspace may include the following attributes.

* workspace name
* location (city/state/country)
* workspace type
* capacity
* amenities
* pricing
* availability
* partner owner

---

# 5. Booking Process

The standard booking flow is:

1. User searches workspace
2. User selects workspace
3. User selects date and duration
4. User confirms booking
5. User completes payment
6. Booking confirmation is generated

---

# 6. Payment System

Payments are processed through integrated payment gateways.

Common fields include:

* payment ID
* order ID
* amount
* currency
* payment status
* booking reference

Possible payment statuses:

* pending
* completed
* failed
* refunded

Invoices are generated after successful payment.

---

# 7. Revenue Model

FlashSpace generates revenue through:

* workspace booking commissions
* subscription plans
* partner listing fees
* affiliate partnerships

Revenue may be shared between the platform and space partners.

# 10. Support System

# 11. Data Privacy and Security

The platform must never expose sensitive information such as:

* user passwords
* payment card numbers
* authentication tokens
* personal identification documents

Sensitive financial data should always be summarized.

---

# 12. AI Assistant Guidelines

The FlashSpace AI Assistant must:

1. Identify user role before answering.
2. Use structured responses.
3. Ask clarification when queries are unclear.
4. Provide accurate workspace recommendations.
5. Protect sensitive user data.
